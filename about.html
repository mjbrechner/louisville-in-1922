About

This project is designed to showcase a snapshot of the history of Louisville using a curated set of local newspaper articles that caught my attention. I decided to focus my attention on the year 1922, a full century prior to the creation of this website, and long enough ago that the relevant newspaper content would be safely in the public domain. I thought that a good way to approach the project would be to display a daily article corresponding to whatever day of the year the user happened to visit the site. It is a somewhat time consuming process to transcribe each article, but I hope to eventually have one for every day of the year.
